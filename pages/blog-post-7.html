✨ Nutrify Skin

September 2023

We all want beautiful flawless skin… that's a given. But at what cost are we willing to go & give up our "faces" to TikTok trends?

Your face is not a TikTok trend.

And most importantly, it was not made solely for altering purposes. Because let's face it — quick fixes or tweakments (Botox, fillers, etc.) even if they give quick results, they will never heal the deep rooted cause of your issue — which is your "insecurity" about your looks. It's a train that once you hop on, you will most likely never get off of. Before you know it you'll turn into somewhat of an addict — as soon as the effect goes away you'll be racing to get your touch up injection, & the cycle goes on and on and on…

What I Am All For

Let me be clear — I'm all for pampering yourself & having a full on spa day, facials, topical treatments — yes, 1000%! All of these embrace your skin & face all while providing that little pampering effect we all need & should indulge in from time to time.

But that's all there is to it — a little pick-me-up without tweaking or injecting anything. Why? Because it celebrates YOU as you are Now! Without trying to improvise your face or alter your natural look. It gives us the opportunity to set aside a little "Me Time" — a chance to connect with ourselves, all while giving us a little dose of daily TLC.

The Nutritious Glow

Nothing beats a healthy from-within glow. It's Real… it's Genuine… it's Nutritious — & it will show on your face. It's a type of confidence no syringe or tweakment will ever give you.

Best of all? Your results are permanent — no touch ups needed.

That's exactly what I'm about — connecting & becoming more in tune with yourself just as you are, enhancing your natural skin & giving it the best nutrition (inner care) & routine (outer care) for it to look its absolute best.

Introducing Nutrify "Skincare"

Which is exactly why I created the Nutrify Skincare service — a holistic approach to skin health that puts nutrition first. Whether you're dealing with acne, pigmentation, dryness, or just want to optimize your glow, we start from within and build outward.
