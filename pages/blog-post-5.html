🌸 Spring Reset

April 2023

Springtime is finally upon us bringing with it all the "spring refresh/reset" vibes. Driving us full force into freshening up our space — from home decor all the way to our closets.

But I want you to bring that energy to your kitchen & hone in on it with that same spring "reset" theme. Let's first examine the fridge & pantry, the snack shelf or cabinet, etc. Take a look at your own kitchen — what kind of food do you have in there?

Step 1 — Evaluate

Begin your evaluation by asking yourself:

  • Can you make healthy meals from the ingredients you have on hand at the moment?
  • Do you know whether that specific food item you usually go for is healthy or if it's processed? Is there a healthier alternative?
  • What about expiration dates?

Step 2 — Sift Through

Start the sifting through process & determine what needs to go & what you will decide to keep. An expired item will be thrown out — but there might also be items that you no longer want to keep in the house even if their expiration date is still far. Those will be the items to eliminate from your fridge/pantry & possibly replace with better options.

Step 3 — Go Shopping

The 3rd & last step is to go shopping for the food items that are currently missing from your Fridge/Pantry. This will make it much easier for you to prep healthier meals or have healthier snack options on hand at home.

Go all out… Springify your kitchen & hit ReSeT on your Fridge/Pantry!!

Think of this as your annual kitchen audit — a chance to realign your food environment with your health goals. Your environment shapes your choices more than your willpower does. When your kitchen is stocked with wholesome options, healthy eating becomes the path of least resistance.

So this spring — don't just declutter your closet. Declutter your pantry too. Your future self will thank you!
